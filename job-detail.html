Marketing Manager

Buffalo, NY

Full Time

$120K

Deadline: Feb 1, 2024

Say Yes Buffalo is a non-profit organization dedicated to ensuring the success of Buffalo's youth by providing access to education, resources, and support. We are seeking a talented and passionate Marketing Manager to join our team. As the Marketing Manager at Say Yes Buffalo, you will play a crucial role in developing and implementing marketing strategies that effectively promote our organization's mission and programs. This is a unique opportunity to make a meaningful impact on the lives of Buffalo's students and families.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and execute comprehensive marketing strategies to raise awareness of Say Yes Buffalo's mission, programs, and impact within the community.
  • Collaborate with the leadership team to define and implement the organization's marketing goals and objectives.
  • Manage all aspects of Say Yes Buffalo's digital presence, including website content, social media channels, email marketing, and online advertising campaigns.
  • Create engaging and persuasive content for various marketing channels, ensuring consistency in brand messaging and positioning.
  • Conduct market research and analyze data to identify target audiences, market trends, and opportunities for program expansion.
  • Plan and coordinate events, community outreach initiatives, and public relations efforts to enhance Say Yes Buffalo's visibility and community engagement.

Qualifications:

  1. Bachelor's degree in marketing, communications, or a related field. A master's degree is a plus.
  2. Strong understanding of marketing principles, strategies, and tactics, with a demonstrated ability to develop and execute successful marketing campaigns.
  3. Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to create compelling content for different marketing channels and target audiences.
  4. Proficiency in digital marketing platforms, including social media management tools, content management systems, and email marketing software.
  5. Experience in data analysis and familiarity with marketing analytics tools to measure campaign performance and ROI.
  6. Ability to work effectively in a team environment, collaborating with cross-functional teams and stakeholders.

Join our team and contribute to the transformative work we do to empower Buffalo's youth and build a brighter future for our community. Apply today and make a difference with Say Yes Buffalo!
